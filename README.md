Portal Paradox: https://ironsakit121.itch.io/portal-paradox

Portal Paradox is a chilling first-person horror game that traps you in an endless loop.

Each corridor presents you with a question and two possible answers. Choose correctly to move forward—but make a mistake, and you'll be sent back to the beginning.

Can you break the cycle, or will you be trapped forever?

The HTML version may have a much lower graphics than the .exe version, but still good for trying the product, for better experience download it.

MOVEMENT:

- WASD TO MOVE

- E TO INTERACT

- ESC FOR SETTINGS
